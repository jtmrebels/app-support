Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

  • Restoring purchases: If you purchased a subscription or lifetime unlock, look for Restore Purchases in the app’s settings or paywall.
  • Subscriptions: Subscriptions are managed by Apple. You can view, change, or cancel in iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Billing issues: Apple handles billing directly. If a charge looks incorrect, check your Apple purchase history.
  • App not working: Try force closing the app, reopening, and ensuring you’re on the latest version from the App Store.
